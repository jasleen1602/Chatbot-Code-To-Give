ABOUT

BuzzWomen bot is an AI enabled chatbot that simulates human-like conversations with users via text messages and voice recognition on chat. Its key task is to enable women to ignite their personal and collective power. It supports more than 50 Languages.

Demo

Technology

Our goal is to make AI accesible to all


Web Design

Our approach to website design is to create a website that makes the chat-bot accessible to all while ensuring ease of use and simplicity of people of all age groups and communities

Artificial Intelligence

Training the chat-bot requires Natural Language processing to understand users' queries and a suitable Deep learning model and neural networks to train it for responses

Backend and API

Flask is used as the backend framework of web-application in python. Chat-bot is integrated in whatsapp using API and can be integrated to other messengers to reach wider audience

Features

  1. AI trained chatbot using Deep Learning Model
  2. Multilingual Bot supporting more than 50 Languages
  3. Integration in whatsapp and other messengers
  4. Voice-assistant and speech recognition to support illiterate and PWDs
  5. User-friendly interface and manual
